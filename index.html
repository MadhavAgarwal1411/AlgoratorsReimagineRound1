    Raw Comb Honey
    Raw comb honey is a pure and unprocessed form of honey that includes the edible beeswax comb. It's harvested directly from the hive, showcasing the natural hexagonal cells created by bees to store honey. This type of honey is prized for its delicate flavor and texture, often enjoyed as a gourmet treat. It retains all the natural enzymes, pollen, and antioxidants found in honey, making it a sought-after choice for those who appreciate its unaltered sweetness and health benefits.
